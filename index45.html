Swiss / Grid
Interactive Typography
01
Architecture
+
Form follows function. The structure dictates the layout. In the Swiss style, grid systems are not merely a tool for alignment, but the primary aesthetic driver of the design.
02
Typography
+
The elimination of ornamentation. We rely strictly on the mathematical kerning and leading of sans-serif typefaces to create hierarchy and direct the user's eye across the page.
03
Whitespace
+
Negative space is as critical as the ink itself. Unrestrained blank areas create tension and allow the massive typographical features to breathe and command the viewport unconditionally.
